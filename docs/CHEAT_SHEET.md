# Cheat Sheet

This cheat sheet provides the "answers" to the Data Quests in your README. Keep this handy during your 20–30 minute session to help kids who get stuck or to call out "World Records" during your presentation. [1]

## Level 1: Basic Navigation

* **The Tiny Titan** (Height < 1'8" / 0.5m, Attack > 100):
  * Answers: Mankey (1'8" / 0.5m / 80 Atk — close!), Cranidos (2'11" / 0.9m / 125 Atk), or Kartana (1'0" / 0.3m / 181 Atk).
  * Teacher Tip: Look for "Baby" or "Small" Pokémon that look angry or have big claws.
* **Speed King** (Fastest Type Average):
  * Answer: Flying or Electric.
  * Teacher Tip: Many kids guess Fire, but Flying usually wins because of its high average speed across all evolutions.

## Level 2: Comparing Data

* **The Shape-Shifter** (Describe Radar Chart shapes):
  * Snorlax: A massive "blob" shape with a huge spike toward HP.
  * Pikachu: A small, "spiky" triangle pointing toward Speed.
  * Teacher Tip: Explain that a "balanced" Pokémon like Mewtwo or Arceus looks like a large, even hexagon.
* **Above the Bar** (HP vs. Global Average):
  * Answer: Global Average HP is around 70.
  * Teacher Tip: Show them that even "strong" Pokémon like Gengar have below-average HP because they are "Glass Cannons".

## Level 3: Finding Outliers

* **The Rule Breaker** (Weight > 440 lbs / 200kg, tiny < 1'8" / 0.5m):
  * Answer: Cosmoem (2,204 lbs / 999.9kg and only 4 inches / 0.1m tall!).
  * Teacher Tip: This is the ultimate "Rule Breaker"—as heavy as a car but small as a ball.
* **The Specialist** (One HUGE stat spike):
  * Answer: Shuckle (Massive Defense) or Regieleki (World Record Speed).
  * Teacher Tip: A specialist is like a "Tool"—you only bring them out for one specific job!

## Presentation "Pro-Moves"

* The "Speed of Light" Fact: Tell them Regieleki is the fastest Pokémon in the data, with a Speed of 200—that's faster than a jet plane!.
* The "Heavyweight" Fact: Cosmoem and Celesteela are the heaviest (2,204 lbs / 999.9kg), but Cosmoem is only 4" (0.1m) tall! It's as heavy as a car but small as a ball. [8, 9, 11, 15]
