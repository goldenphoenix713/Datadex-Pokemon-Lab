# Data-Dex: Ultimate Stat Lab — Cheat Sheet

**Live App:** [datadex.famruiz.com](https://datadex.famruiz.com)

This cheat sheet provides the "answers" to the Data Quests. Keep this handy during your 20–30 minute session to help kids who get stuck or to call out "World Records" during your presentation.

## Level 1: Basic Navigation

* **The Tiny Titan** (Height < 1'8" / 0.5m, Attack > 100):
  * **Answer:** **Kartana**. It is only 1'0" (0.3m) tall but has a massive **181 Attack**!
  * **Teacher Tip:** Tell the kids that Kartana is made of paper but is sharper than a samurai sword.
* **Speed King** (Fastest Type Average):
  * **Answer:** **Electric**.
  * **Teacher Tip:** Many kids guess Fire or Flying, but Electric consistently has the highest average speed across all its species.

## Level 2: Comparing Data

* **The Shape of Stats** (Mew vs. Deoxys Normal Forme):
  * **Answer:** **Mew** is a perfect hexagon (100 in every stat). **Deoxys** is a "Glass Cannon" with huge spikes in Attack and Speed, but almost no Defense.
  * **Teacher Tip:** Explain that even though they have the same "Total" power (600), they play completely differently!
* **Above the Bar** (HP vs. Global Average):
  * **Answer:** The Global Average HP is approximately **70**.
  * **Teacher Tip:** Show them that even "strong" Pokémon like Gengar actually have below-average HP.
* **Trainer vs Pokémon** (Side-by-side):
  * **Answer:** (Subjective) Most kids will be taller than a Pikachu but much lighter than a Charizard!

## Level 3: Finding Outliers

* **Deceptive Appearances** (Tiny but ultra-heavy):
  * **Answer:** **Cosmoem**. It is only 4 inches (0.1m) tall but weighs **2,204 lbs (999.9kg)**!
  * **Teacher Tip:** That’s as heavy as a car, but it can fit in your hand. It's the densest Pokémon in the universe.
* **The Unbreakable Wall** (Highest Defense + Special Defense):
  * **Answer:** **Shuckle**. It has **230 Defense** and **230 Special Defense**, but only 10 Attack.
  * **Teacher Tip:** Shuckle is the ultimate tank—it can't hurt you, but you can't hurt it either!
